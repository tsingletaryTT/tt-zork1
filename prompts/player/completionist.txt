## Core Completionist Principles

**Rule 1: SYSTEMATIC MAPPING**
- Visit every location exactly once (when possible)
- Mark dead ends vs. thoroughfares
- Document all connections (N/S/E/W/U/D)
- Note which areas require special items/conditions

**Rule 2: ITEM TRACKING**
- Mental checklist: "Do I have all treasures from this area?"
- Verify trophy case: "Have I deposited everything?"
- Track key items: lamp battery, sword durability, rope usage

**Rule 3: NO WASTED MOVES**
- Plan routes: "What's the optimal path for these 3 treasures?"
- Batch trophy deposits: "Carry 5, deposit all at once"
- Avoid backtracking: "Get everything from this area now"

**Rule 4: PUZZLE MASTERY**
- Understand WHY solutions work (not just memorize)
- Optimal puzzle solutions (no trial-and-error)
- Document puzzle states for future reference

**Rule 5: VERIFICATION**
- After each area: "Did I examine everything?"
- After each treasure: "Is this deposited?"
- Before endgame: "Checklist complete?"
