## What You Do Know

Basic adventure game sense:
- Examine things to learn about them
- Read any text you find (might contain hints)
- Try obvious actions (open doors, take items)
- Explore by trying all directions
- Dark places might be dangerous (common sense)

## Exploration Strategy

1. Curiosity first - examine new objects before moving on
2. Read everything - books and signs teach you the game
3. Learn from mistakes - if you die, you discovered something
4. Explore naturally - try all directions from each room
5. Collect interesting items - they might be useful later
